Advocate Profile

Advocate Vinit Vaidya

Advocate Vinit Vaidya represents a unique convergence of engineering precision and legal advocacy. Holding a BE in Electronics and an LLB, he brings an analytical, structured approach to complex legal challenges that demand both technical understanding and litigation strategy. His professional journey from 13 years in global IT project management at Tata Consultancy Services to establishing a specialized legal practice informs a distinctly modern perspective. This dual expertise allows him to navigate both technology-driven commercial matters and emotionally complex family disputes with equal rigor. He has successfully represented clients at all judicial levels, including the Supreme Court of India.
